# 📊 Tesla Social Media Analytics for Strategic Branding

## 📌 Overview
This project develops a **Social Media Intelligence Solution in Excel** for Tesla, consolidating raw marketing data from multiple platforms (Twitter/X, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn) into actionable insights. The analysis empowers Tesla’s digital marketing team to make **data-driven decisions** on branding, campaign planning, and ROI optimization.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify which content resonates most with Tesla’s audience.  
- Measure ROI of advertising spend across platforms.  
- Analyze how product launches and campaigns influence follower growth.  
- Recommend strategies to improve community loyalty and advocacy.  

## 📂 Datasets
1. **Posts Dataset** → Post-level metrics (likes, shares, comments, impressions, hashtags).  
2. **Engagement Summary Dataset** → Weekly aggregated engagement, follower growth, ad spend.  
3. **Campaign Metadata Dataset** → Campaign objectives, spend, impressions, engagement uplift.  

## 🛠️ Tasks Completed
- **Task 1: Data Preprocessing & Cleaning**  
  Removed duplicates, standardized dates/platforms, formatted numeric fields, split hashtags.  
- **Task 2: Engagement Analysis**  
  Calculated engagement rates, identified top posts, analyzed hashtags via pivot tables.  
- **Task 3: Platform Analysis**  
  Compared engagement vs. ad spend, follower growth rates, and platform ROI.  
- **Task 4: Hashtag & Content Strategy**  
  Evaluated hashtag frequency and performance by content type; recommended platform-specific strategies.  
- **Task 5: Campaign Effectiveness**  
  Measured impressions, clicks, ROI, and follower growth impact per campaign.  
- **Task 6: Follower Retention & Loyalty**  
  Visualized weekly growth, applied moving averages, correlated ad spend with growth.  
- **Task 7: Video Summary**  
  Presented findings and actionable recommendations in a video explanation.  

## 📈 Key Insights
- **Top-performing hashtags**: #Cybertruck, #EVAdoption, #TeslaEnergy.  
- **Content type priorities**:  
  - Videos → Best on YouTube (product demos, Elon talks).  
  - Images → Strong on Instagram (car designs, interiors).  
  - Carousels → Useful for feature comparisons.  
- **Platform strategy**: Twitter/X drives announcements, YouTube boosts demos; multi-platform presence remains valuable.  
- **Campaign ROI**: Cybertruck launch generated the highest engagement uplift.
